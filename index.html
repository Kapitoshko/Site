Why choose Akash?

  • Computing power market (lease occurs from providers connected to the Akash marketplace). 
  • Technical support in Telegram and Discord chats 
  • Payment in cryptocurrency (AKT token). 
  • Lack of any registration (install Akash CLI or UI, top up your AKT balance and you can start deploying your services!) 
  • No bans for mining\using a communication channel. 
  • Saving money (power 2 cpu, 4 RAM, 100 Gb SSD from $6 per month). 

    You can go on listing the benefits of Akash for a long time, but it's easier to just try it!
